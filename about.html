Valorant is a free-to-play first-person shooter video game developed by Riot Games. The game is set in a near-future Earth where players take on the roles of "agents" with unique abilities, and participate in matches with two teams of five players each. The objective of the game is to plant or defuse a bomb or to eliminate the other team before time runs out. The game has a tactical focus, with players needing to communicate and coordinate their actions to win matches. The game features a wide range of weapons and equipment, as well as a ranking system for competitive play.

Currently I am a top player in the game ranking in the top .01% of players in the North American Region. I have invested countless hours in hopes of reaching the top rank of "Radiant", but to no avail. I will continue to strive to reach the top where only the elite stand.
